Hello! I'm Thamidul Islam Tonmoy. I have completed my B.Sc. in EEE from Bangladesh University of Engineering & Technology (BUET). Currently I'm working as a Research Assistant in DSP Research Lab, BUET. My research interests lie in the areas of biomedical signal processing and biomedical instrumentation. I am particularly interested in applying the knowledge of computer vision and deep learning in biomedical signal processing and implement them to develop biomedical instruments and imaging techniques for more robust computer aided diagnosis system.
Apart from these chores, I love traveling, photography and food hunting.

 

Research Interests
  • Signal Processing, Video and Image Processing, Medical Imaging, Biomedical Signal Processing and Machine Learning.

 

Research Experience
