Hello! I'm Thamidul Islam Tonmoy. I have completed my B.Sc. in EEE from Bangladesh University of Engineering & Technology (BUET). Currently I'm working as a Research Assistant in DSP Research Lab, BUET. My research interests lie in the areas of biomedical image and signal processing. I am particularly interested in applying the knowledge of computer vision and deep learning in biomedical image and signal processing to develop biomedical imaging techniques for more robust computer aided diagnosis system.

 

Research Interests

Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Biomedical Image Processing, Biomedical Signal Processing.

 

Research Experience

  1. Image Processing with Convolutional Neural Network (on going)
    In this research, we are trying to remove noise/artifacts introduced by adverse environmental condition during capturing image using convolutional neural network.

  2. Traffic Sign Detection and Recognition
    Worked on the winning model of IEEE VIP Cup 2017 to enhance the robustness of the detection system which uses state of the art neural networks to tackle the challenge.

  3. Breast Cancer Classification from Histopathological Image
    In this undergrad thesis research, we explored the power of deep neural networks in two different domains (image and DCT) to classify breast histopathological images.

 

Publications
